About Us
Nigerian Community Leeds (NCL) is a registered Charity based in Leeds, West Yorkshire.

NCL exists to support members of the public who are Nigerian by birth, ancestry and descent, marriage, naturalisation or adoption in and around Leeds. We cater for people of all ages and enable them to reach their full potential as individuals contributing to health & socio-economic wellbeing in the city of Leeds.

Excel@NCL
